## Not just a list of names, but a family encyclopedia

In the past, family trees were drawn on parchment scrolls or cumbersome documents that faded over time. Later came genealogy software packed with details, expensive monthly subscriptions, and systems that were simply too complicated to use.

Toldotay changes the rules of the game and creates a bridge between generations. It is not just a data system, but a home for all the stories you heard from Grandma in the kitchen and all the yellowing photographs tucked away in old family albums.

## How does it work? A gift that becomes a family experience

When you build a family tree in our system as a gift, you are actually creating a unifying experience for the entire extended family:

- **A dedicated viewing dashboard for the whole family:** Every grandchild, great-grandchild, and relative receives a private and secure link. With one click, they enter a beautifully designed family dashboard where they can navigate the branches of the tree, discover relatives, and learn about their roots.
- **A personal profile for every family member:** Each person in the tree receives a personal card that includes photos, important dates, and a biography. This is the place to preserve immigration stories, achievements, and the unique legacy of grandparents.
- **A clean and clear top-down design:** Unlike clunky systems, Toldotay presents the chain of generations in a smooth, visual, and easy-to-follow way, specially designed to preserve accurate lineage.

## The perfect gift for family occasions

Imagine grandparents sitting at their birthday celebration while, on the big screen, the magnificent family tree you built for them suddenly appears. They see their own grandparents, then move down to their children, and all the way to the youngest great-grandchild born just last month. The emotion in the room during moments like these is impossible to describe. It is living, breathing proof that their journey bore fruit.
